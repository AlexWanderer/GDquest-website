Do you want to become a better 2d game artist?

You came to the right place! Using Krita, a free and open source painting application, Game Art Quest will show you both essential concepts and concrete techniques professionals use on a daily basis.

Click here to download Krita !

Game Art Quest is a premium training series in 3 volumes. It was funded on Kickstarter by 424 generous backers!

[FREE] Volume 1 - Learn to use Krita for professional game art.

This training will show you the tools Krita has to offer to game artists. It covers the tools that you need to work efficiently, as well as some workflow related tips. It is completely free, thanks to the Kickstarter backers! It is available on both Udemy and Youtube.

Volume 2 - Painterly game art and side-scrolling games

In this second training, we will take a closer look at the game creation pipeline from a game artist’s standpoint. We will create static assets for a side scrolling video game with a painterly art style, inspired by the work of VanillaWare (Odin Sphere, Muramasa) and Moon Studios (Ori and the Blind Forest).

Volume 3 - Cel shaded game art and isometric games

This second volume focuses on isometric game with a cel shaded art style
